Founding

In 2022, Billie Jean King Enterprises and the Mark Walter Group created the Professional Women's Hockey Player Association with professional women players and purchased the Premier Hockey Federation. At the time, this was the leading sector for women's professional hockey. Before the PHF, there were many other women's professional leagues, mainly within North America but also making it the whole way to China. Every league failed in some way, but everyone still wanted a league to exist, which is exactly what this one is trying to do.

By August of 2023, The Professional Women's Hockey League was announced, with 6 original teams. Looking at the NHL and the former women's leagues, there is overlap, but for a reason.

In April of 2025, two more teams were added to the roster, Vancouver and Seattle.
